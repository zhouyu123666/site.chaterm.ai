## Frost & Sullivan Report: Best Practices in China's Generative AI Industry in 2025

Frost & Sullivan, in conjunction with HeadLeopard Research Institute, released the "Best Application Practices in China's Generative AI Industry in 2025." The report points out that thanks to the continued boom in artificial intelligence research, the total number of AI papers published continues to grow, the performance of AI in rigorous benchmark tests continues to improve, and the inference cost to achieve specific performance levels has significantly decreased. Generative AI (GenAI) has greatly improved the efficiency and quality of content generation, and Agentic AI is further creating new and deeper value based on GenAI by introducing autonomy, goal orientation, and multi-step execution capabilities. The report analyzes high-quality overseas generative AI cases and selects the best practices in China's generative AI industry for 2025 from eight industries across four dimensions.
